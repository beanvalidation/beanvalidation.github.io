Adding proposal section and first proposal

by Emmanuel Bernard on 21 Oct 2011 proposal site

We have introduced a new section of the website called proposals. This will include wiki-style, work in progress proposals for various features being worked on.

Check out the first proposal page describing ideas and open questions on how to propose dependency injection in ConstraintValidator instances.
